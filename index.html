About Us

SmyleHacks is proud to partner with SMYLE, a nonprofit organization devoted to giving back to the community. Our hackathon is not only for students to gain coding expertise but also to receive an opportunity to give back to their own communities. In the hackathon, programmers will collaborate with others to build innovative projects that help their community.
